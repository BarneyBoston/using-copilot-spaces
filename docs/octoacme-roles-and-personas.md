# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises. It covers both core delivery roles and supporting roles that contribute to successful project outcomes.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
Responsible for user experience research, design, and validation. Ensures that products are intuitive, accessible, and meet user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Validate designs with users and stakeholders
- Collaborate with Developers on implementation feasibility
- Work with Product Managers to translate user needs into features
- Maintain design systems and consistency across products

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered, accessible designs
- Reduce friction in user journeys
- Validate design decisions with evidence and research

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings and recommendations
- Prototype walkthroughs and design handoffs

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate on requirements and feature definitions; translate user insights into product priorities
- **Developers**: Partner during implementation to ensure design feasibility and quality; review implementations
- **Project Managers**: Provide design estimates and dependencies; participate in planning sessions

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Gathers, analyzes, and interprets data to inform decision-making, measure success, and identify opportunities for improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key metrics and KPIs
- Analyze user behavior, product performance, and business outcomes
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Provide data-driven insights for prioritization and decision-making
- Support A/B testing and experimentation
- Identify data quality issues and gaps

### Goals
- Enable evidence-based decision making
- Measure and communicate project impact
- Identify trends and opportunities through data

### Typical Communication
- Metric definitions and dashboard reviews
- Analysis findings and recommendations
- Data requirements and dependencies

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- **Product Managers**: Define success metrics; analyze feature performance and user behavior; inform backlog prioritization
- **Project Managers**: Provide reporting data; monitor project health metrics; identify risks through data signals
- **Developers**: Collaborate on instrumentation and data collection; validate data quality

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
Owns infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, deployment automation, and production monitoring. Enables reliable, efficient software delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure and cloud resources
- Automate deployment processes
- Monitor production health and performance
- Respond to incidents and implement reliability improvements
- Ensure security and compliance in infrastructure

### Goals
- Minimize deployment friction and time-to-production
- Maximize system reliability and uptime
- Enable developers to ship safely and quickly

### Typical Communication
- Pipeline and infrastructure status
- Post-mortems and incident reports
- Infrastructure capacity and planning

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- **Developers**: Enable build/test automation; provide deployment tools; collaborate on production troubleshooting
- **Project Managers**: Coordinate release planning and deployment windows; communicate infrastructure dependencies
- **Product Managers**: Provide production metrics and availability data

---

## Stakeholder Liaison

### Role Summary
Serves as a bridge between the core delivery team and external stakeholders (Support, Sales, Marketing, Legal, etc.). Ensures consistent communication and manages stakeholder expectations.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate stakeholder updates and communication
- Gather and triage feedback from external teams
- Manage change requests and prioritization with stakeholders
- Facilitate stakeholder meetings and reviews
- Document decisions and action items
- Escalate critical issues to appropriate teams

### Goals
- Maintain stakeholder alignment and satisfaction
- Reduce interruptions to the core delivery team
- Ensure timely, accurate communication

### Typical Communication
- Stakeholder newsletters and updates
- Cross-functional meeting coordination
- Feedback synthesis and prioritization

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- **Product Managers**: Relay stakeholder feedback and requirements; coordinate on prioritization decisions
- **Project Managers**: Align on project status and timelines; escalate risks and blockers
- **Developers**: Shield from context-switching; provide consolidated requirements and feedback
